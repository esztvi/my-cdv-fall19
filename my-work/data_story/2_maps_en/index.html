For the purposes of this project, "Hungarians" will be used as a term to describe the ETHNIC group, and not those living in the present-day Hungarain nation. This distinction is vital in order to understand and contextualize the content of this project. These historical maps illustrate the numerous (notable) changes to the country's border in relation to present borders as later shown, migrations of Hungarians occurred when the borders were vastly different from what they are today. Essentially, people once defined as "Hungarian nationals" at the time of emigration would still be ethnically Hungarian despite moving from an area that is present day Romania, Croatia, Slovakia, Austria, or Slovenia.
